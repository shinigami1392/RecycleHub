Welcome to Recycle Hub!

We bring you a vision of cleaner planet and help you understand the importance and need for recycle.

About Our Project

  • 1 The world needs attention!
    With increasing garbage footprint per person, we need a system which helps people realize how much their daily wasteful habits affects the environment.
  • 2 Encourage yourself to recycle garbage
    Human beings always have chosen to believe in metrics to gauge the effect of their actions. The mission of our system is to quantify the amount of garbage a person creates through his daily buying habits
  • 3 Bring home some extra cash
    Once a person is able to visualize the effect of his habits, we also aim to provide them ways to recycle the garbage and make him/her realize how much profit they could make through a little bit of proactiveness.
